üìã Quick Reference - Watering Priority

Check DAILY: Macho Fern, Majesty Palm (these HATE drying out)

Check every 3-4 days: Cat Palm, Fern Accent, Roebellini

Check weekly: Spider Plant, Money Trees, Bamboo Palm, Parlor Palm

Check every 2-3 weeks: Ponytail Palm, Cast Iron Plants (drought tolerant)

üí° Grow Light Tips

Most of these plants will do well under grow lights. Place demanding plants (Majesty Palm, Macho Fern) closest to the light source. The Cast Iron Plants and Parlor Palm can be furthest away - they're low-light champs.
